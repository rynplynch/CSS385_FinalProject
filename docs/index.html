About the Project

Seas & Skies: A naval 3D action game.


Description: Seas & Skies is a 3D action team vs. team game where the player must defeat the enemy using either ship or plane! They must reduce the other teams tickets to win.
Influences: Inspired by games such as War Thunder, World of Warships, with elements from Battlefield.



Features List:


  • Dynamic Combat: Battle on sea or in the air, switching between boats and planes.
  • Upgrades: Upgrade your vehicle with gold to give you the edge in combat over the enemy.
  • Weapons: Use a variety of weapon types to dominate the opposing team.
  • AI: The enemy will fight back, and they are formitable.
  • Ticket System: Reduce the enemy teams tickets by destroying them in combat.


    • Team Reflection

    Reflection: One thing our team did really well was dividing tasks. We took on a project with all sorts of plan and idea on how to tackle it. This made the project seemed impossible to complete in 10 weeks of time. Our team had to have weekly discussions to re-evaluate our current position on tasks and changes to the project. Often times we had to scrap some features and ideas due to time constraint. Following every team discussion, we all decide on what we are going to do next and remain focus on that task. Evidences to prove this is through the usages of the Kanban board in GitHub, every person focusing on their own branch in GitHub, and constant communication through Discord.

    With more time: If we had another 2 weeks to work on the game, our 3 highest priority would be to focus on implementing multiplayer, refining and smoothing out the collisions between vehicles, and adding more vehicle and weapon types.

    Playtesting Reports



    May 8 2024


    Alpha Testing

    Summary:

    The testers were introduced the plane and boat mechanics during the in-class alpha play session. They got a few minutes with the game and gave their initial feedback. The biggest area of concern raised by the playtesters were the intuitiveness and feel of the boat. We took the feedback from this to implement a new boat system and more intuitive controls.

    May 15 2024


    Beta Testing

    Summary:

    During the beta playtest, we showed off the ticket system, gold system and game loop. They got a few minutes with the game and gave their initial feedback. The biggest area of concern raised during this play session was the need for better visuals, UI, and enemies. We addressed these concerns by adding enemy AI and an overhauled UI system to complete the basic game flow.

    May 27 2024


    Private Beta Testing

    Summary:

    For the private beta test we put our game in the hands of our friends and family. This playtest lasted longer per person as they didn't have the time constraints from the previous tests. Most of the feedback during this test was about the weapons and models for the game. The timing of this test made it difficult to implement all of the changes, but we were able to add one more weapon type and update the models for our vehicles. In the future, we want to implement the mutliplayer mechanic that was so elusive and clean up what we currently have.
